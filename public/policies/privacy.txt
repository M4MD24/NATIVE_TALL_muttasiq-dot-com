Muttasiq Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Muttasiq app and website handle user and device data.

1) Scope
- This policy applies to the Muttasiq web app and NativePHP mobile builds that use the same codebase.

2) What We Collect
- We do not require account registration for normal app usage.
- We do not collect payment data inside the app.
- We store user preferences locally (for example reading and display settings) in browser/device storage.

3) Network Usage
- The app may request athkar content from configured endpoints (for example: https://muttasiq.com/api/athkar).
- Standard technical request metadata (such as IP address and user agent) may be processed by hosting/network infrastructure.

4) Admin Access
- The administration panel is for project maintainers only.
- In NativePHP runtime builds, admin routes are disabled and not available to end users.

5) Data Sharing
- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not share user data with third parties for advertising.

6) Data Retention
- Local app settings remain on the user's browser/device unless deleted by the user or by uninstalling/clearing app data.

7) Children
- This app is not designed to collect personal information from children.

8) Security
- We apply reasonable technical measures to protect the service, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

9) Changes to This Policy
- We may update this policy when app behavior or legal requirements change.
- The latest version will always be published at /policies/privacy.txt.

10) Contact
- Project discussions: https://github.com/GoodM4ven/NATIVE_TALL_muttasiq-dot-com/discussions
